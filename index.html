What happens next

These removals do not go in the June 30 SAR. NTIA reviews the change through a single consolidated Post-Award Change, and only after that SAR has been submitted, reviewed, and approved. VA transmittal; FAQ 9.8

Any project shedding more than the smaller of 20% or 200 (CAIs included) needs a New No BEAD Locations Post-Award Change with NTIA approval, not a self-service SAR entry. FAQ 9.7, p.52; Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance, p.4

Each affected project needs a budget modification to reflect the reduced footprint. Confirm the mechanics and your rebuttal entries with your FPO before submitting. FAQ 9.8; Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance

Scope: this tool covers the NTIA-generated LEO Capacity Subgrant removal list only. It counts and flags scale; it does not route non-LEO discretionary drops. For those, use the scope-change decision tree.
